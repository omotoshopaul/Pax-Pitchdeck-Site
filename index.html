Pax’s Mission.

Pax’s mission is to empower individuals with secure, self-sovereign digital identities built on blockchain technology. We aim to eliminate data breaches, restore privacy, and give people full control of their online identities, creating a transparent, trusted, and decentralized future where identity verification is simple, secure, and truly user-owned.
